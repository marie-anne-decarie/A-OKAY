A-OKAY is nothing without our awesome muses.

Meet the beloved faces of the mid-twenty-first century's most optimistic brand.

MARS

Everyone's favorite adrenaline sports athlete has been a friend of A-OKAY since basically forever! The three-times Statera Cyber Race winner changed history in 2044 when she hit the racetrack decked head-to-toe in A-OKAY gear, proudly representing the brand for the first time ever. Her cheerful and sometimes cheeky attitude inspire us to look on the bright side and have a good time.

    Trivia
  • B-day: December 6 (saggitarius)
  • Favorite food: Pizza WITH pineapple. Argue with the wall.
  • Catchphrase: "Are you even trying?" (said cheekily at competitors)

STELLA B.

She's the trend, she's the moment, she's unforgettable. Stella B. has left an indelebile mark in the hearts of millions with her airy pop music and artistry alike. Hypnotizing, stylish, kind-hearted, she embodies through her art a romanticized past, present and future. Her flawless fairy-like and retro-futurist fashion are simply iconic, which is why we are more than excited to have collaborated with her for the next A-OKAY x Stella B. fashion drop. You can't miss out!

    Trivia
  • B-day: August 25 (virgo)
  • Favorite color: purple and a-okay green, obvi
  • Hobbies: writing poetry, cosplaying, painting and baking

BASSLINE KAT

Very little is known about the true identity of Bassline Kat, the mysterious DJ that's been popping up all around town and setting fire to venue after venue (sometimes literally) in the past few months. What we know about them? They have biiiiiig plans for the future, and they enthusiastically accepted to be the newest addition to A-OKAY's multiple faces. Or, helmets. Whatever.

    Trivia
  • B-day: Rumor has it they're a scorpio...
  • Goals and ambitions: Take over the world one banger at the time. Nah seriously.
  • Favorite drink: water.
